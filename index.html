About WidgetDock
Redefining Dock Widgets
WidgetDock Store is the official store for WidgetDock widgets — your curated destination to explore, share, and install powerful extensions that enhance your workflow and personalize your experience.
Discover beautifully designed widgets that combine performance with a sleek, macOS-inspired aesthetic. From productivity tools to stylish utilities, WidgetDock offers something for every user.

WidgetDock Store celebrates creative indie widget developers and is not affiliated with Apple Inc.
